Introduction

Summoner (Su) is the game’s pet class. Typical Su gameplay involves playing at mid to long range while casting your pets’ Photon Arts to do damage, and periodically casting techniques to support your pet.

This guide is intended to provide information on the following to those looking to start playing Summoner:

- Summoner’s Skill Tree

- Subclass Choices for Summoner

- Pet Setup, Pet Candy

- Each Pet’s Usage, Personality, and PAs

- Takts, Rings, & Mag

- Units, Unit SSAs for Summoner

- techniques

Pros/Cons of Summoner


Pros:
Great survivability due to Dear Master High damage potential Handles most content well via access to multiple types of pets After initial setup, pets require next to no investment or upkeep Cons: Initial pet setup is lengthy, & tedious Powerful pet candy is sometimes locked behind old, seasonal content or the player shop Building up a repertoire of pets takes a long time Initial pet setup is expensive, & resource intensive, especially so for someone new to the game
