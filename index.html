Introduction

Summoner is a versatile class that makes use of a variety of different pets, each with their own unique move sets, strengths, and weaknesses. Great Summoners are able to manage their pets’ HP, positioning, and buffs, identify what pets best fit the current quest at hand, and make use of an array of techniques to support their pets, all while kicking out high damage.

The main detriment to Summoner is the notable meseta and resource costs associated with gearing pets for the first time, but it’s not too bad to overcome through weekly missions and a little patience, so don’t let this stop you from picking up Summoner even as a new player.

Through reading this guide I hope you'll see that there's far more to the class than you might think and hopefully give it a shot!

If you have any questions about Summoner or this guide itself, you can hit me up on Discord at airoh.#6794 or in the Summoner Discord.

If you’re playing on the North American servers, look out for purple text highlighting translations and information pertaining specifically to NA.
