Skill Rings

A list of recommended left and right skill rings for Summoner.

All rings should be grinded to +20 unless stated otherwise.

  • L/ Rings
  • R/ Rings
Note: Left rings can also be added to 12 or 13★ Units, allowing you to equip up to 4 left rings at once.
L/Defense Tech
Shortens the animation on your mirage step, and provides you with guard frames upon charging a technique. A shorter step allows you to spend less time dodging and more time hitting stuff. If you make only one left ring, make this one.


L/Jumping Dodge
Enables the Active Skill “Jumping Dodge” to be equipped to your Subpalette. Activate to instantly gain altitude. During this jump, you are invincible. 5s cooldown. Allows you to vertically reposition yourself to more easily lock on to enemies, or enemy weak points that are high up. Can also be used before Maron Strike to put distance between you and enemies, providing you more time to swap pets after Maron dies.


L/Mag Excite
Increases the power of your mag's auto action and reduces its cooldown. Summoner doesn't have any class rings that increase our damage, so instead, summoner can use this to gain damage. Pairs well with the Megid auto action.


L/Near Auto Target
Pet automatically normal attacks a nearby enemy when you swing your Takt. The main strength of this ring is in mobbing content - your pet will auto attack new crowds of enemies as you walk up to them.

In bossing content however, this ring can be detrimental to you, as it can sometimes result in your pet attacking the wrong part of the boss. For that reason, it can be useful to put this ring in your character's left ring slot, and slot your other rings into your units, so you can unequip this ring at will.




Atlas Tahat Ex 15★ - Eternal War Scherzo - Increase your pet’s power by 6%. Your pet may survive one fatal blow with 1 HP. Restores pet HP at regular intervals afterwards.

Does not prevent a fatal blow on Maron or Melon Strike. Power can be increased to 8% via Luminmech Ether Fragments.
This isn't something you should include on your weapon palette all the time, but this can sometimes be useful for keeping your pet alive during a particularly dangerous fight.



Ceres Cicopus Zara 14★ - Immortal Moment - Gain a power bonus that increases every two hits (6% power at 10 hits). When power bonus is maxed, gain a barrier that grants super armour and 10% damage reduction.

Barrier will fade if you don't land hits on enemies for 15s.
With its 6% power now outdone by multiple other weapons, Zara remains useful only for the super armour it provides.

The main use of Zara's super armour is to prevent you being staggered out of Melon Strike outside of Alter Ego Harmony, but this Takt can be useful in other situations as well.



Milliorazi Orb/Dusk 15★ - Crimson Evening - Grants 6% power and automatically restores 2 PP every second.

Useful for its free PP recovery during downtime in combat, or as a means of improving your PP recovery while normal attacking.

This isn't something to prioritize on your weapon palette, but if you're committing to running one pet with Steady Parfaits for a significant length of time, or you're running a quest with a lot of downtime, this isn't a bad utility to have.
