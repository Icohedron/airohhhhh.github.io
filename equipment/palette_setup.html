Weapon Palette Setup

Through your weapon palette, you'll be able to equip your pets and takts.

Your weapon palette will wildly differ depending on the quest you're doing - different content requires different pets/takts.

To start putting stuff on your weapon palette, navigate to Escape > Open Equipment > Equip Weapons.

Equipping Takts and Pets


To equip a Takt, simply click on one and then click on a slot of your weapon palette.
You can then equip pets and pet PAs in the same way.
