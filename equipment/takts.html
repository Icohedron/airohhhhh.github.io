Takts

Summoner's only weapon type. Takts allow you to equip your pets while also providing you with other bonuses in combat.

  • Core Takts
  • Utility Takts
  • Other Takts


Rykros Staff 14★ - Glance of Darkness - During Alter Ego, Rykros will automatically activate Pet Sympathy, and fire a single-target dark projectile during each pet PA cast.

Projectiles scale only from T-ATK, and don't fire with Maron or Melon Strike. Upgradable via Ultimate Boosters! Further increases the damage of Rykros projectiles.
This Takt is best used alongside pets with quick PAs when fighting a boss or a small crowd (1-3 mobs), and should only be used during Alter Ego.

The pet that best showcases this is Synchro, but Rykros is also useful on Jinga and Rappy.


Liberate Takt 15★ - Liberated Ensemble - Increase critical hit rate by 20%, increase power by 8%, and decrease PP consumption by 20%.
8% power makes your burst damage such as Maron Strike and Aero Spiral even stronger, which is valuable in content that lacks a damage cap.

Liberate's increased power and reduced PP consumption are useful to pets that don't make effective use of Rykros Staff, such as Melon, Redran, or Viola.

Finally, Liberate is great for any pet during Alter Ego's cooldown.



Lightstream Canxion 15★ - Lightstream Melody - Increase power by 5%. Additionally, fully restore your PP when casting a PA or Technique while below 10% PP, and gain 3% power for 30s. This effect has a 120s cooldown.
Upgradable with Luminmech Ether Fragments for an extra 3% damage.
Lightstream is useful as a second Takt on pets that burn PP hard, namely Synchro and Redran, or in any situation where you find yourself out of PP.
