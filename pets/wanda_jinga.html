Wanda/Jinga

This pet excels at dispatching crowds of mobs with great PP efficiency, but falls behind other pets when tasked with fighting a single target for an extended period of time.

Because of their great mobbing capabilities, solid survivability, and passable single target damage output, Wanda or Jinga is definitely the all-rounder pet to focus on first if you're new to the class.

Wanda vs. Jinga

Wanda and Jinga have the same Photon Arts and Personalities, but differ otherwise.

As the evolved form of Wanda, Jinga is the preferred option because of its shorter revive time, lower PA costs, and increased range.

Enjoys Sun

Personality that increases Wanda or Jinga's damage by 12% when the weather is clear.

Wanda and Jinga's preferred base personality.

Fervent

Wanda or Jinga gains a low chance to avoid fatal blows.

When a fatal blow is avoided this way, the pet becomes invincible for a few seconds and gains 15% increased damage for 60 seconds.

Powerful (14★)

Wanda or Jinga gains the effects of Enjoys Sun and Fervent. Also, +5% RDR.

Wanda and Jinga's finalized personality.

Photon Arts

Jinga Assault (5 PP)
Jinga briefly zig-zags in a line before returning to your side.

Sometimes used to hit enemies in a line, but the main use of this PA is to recall Jinga so you can buff it or heal it.



Jinga Break (10 PP)
Jinga dashes in a circle, knocking enemies down.

Your PA of choice for attacking small to mid-sized crowds of mobs.



Jinga Slicer (15 PP)
Jinga performs two wide slashes, hitting enemies in front of it.
