Synchro

Your pet of choice against most bosses because of its extremely high single target damage output. Synchro's attacks have pretty small AOE, so avoid using them against crowds of mobs.

The trade off for dealing such high damage is that Synchro has pretty mediocre HP, low defense, and poor PP recovery on its normal attacks.

Uniquely, Synchro’s PAs also feature a combo system.

60s Revive Time

Devoted

When you switch to Synchro from a dead pet, Synchro gains 12% damage.

This personality's effect is active indefinitely, provided you don't switch to a different pet. Switching pets (even to another Synchro) will remove this buff, requiring you to swap to a dead pet, then switch to Synchro again.

Synchro's preferred base personality.

Cunning

When switching to Synchro from a dead pet, Synchro’s PA costs are reduced by 20%.

Active so long as you don't swap off of Synchro, same as Devoted personality.

Gentle (14★)

Offers the bonuses of Devoted and Cunning as well as +5% RDR.

Synchro's finalized personality.

Maron Strike (5 PP)
Maron's signature PA. Kill your Maron by throwing it at an enemy, dealing damage in an area.

The damage and AOE of Strike increases based on charge level.

Due to Phantom's Tech Short Charge, it's pretty easy to drop a charged Zanverse cast before throwing a fully charged Maron for a free 200k+ Zanverse tick - just make sure not to switch off of the Maron until the Zanverse damage registers.



Maron Shield (5 PP)
Hold your Maron in front of you like a shield.

This can be held to put Maron in harm's way (preventing you from taking damage, and ensuring Maron does), or simply tapped to return Maron to your side. Drains 1 PP every 2s when held.



Maron Chaser (25 PP)
Maron launches a projectile that bounces from enemy to enemy.

Used to aggro multiple enemies when using Maron in a mobbing context.



Maron Curse (40 PP)
Maron launches a slow-moving projectile which applies a DoT for 9.5s.

Solid single-target dps provided the enemy lives long enough for all ticks of Curse to deal damage.



Maron Break (5 PP)
Maron dashes in a circle, dealing damage.

This is your main PA to use while stalling for charge.
